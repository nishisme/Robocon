THEME & RULES

"GREAT URTUU"

The ABU Asia-Pacific Robot Contest (ABU Robocon) is an annual robot competition organized by Asia- Pacific Broadcasting Union (ABU) since 2002. ABU Robocon 2019 is scheduled to be organized on 25 August in the city of Ulanbaatar in Mongolia. The winning team of DD-Robocon 2019 will represent India in Mongolia.

THE GAME

Each country is represented by one team. The task this year is to make two robots (one could be wheel based and another has to be 4 - legged walking robot) within specified dimensions and weight limits to be perform a set of task in 3 minutes over a field of 13 m by 10 m.

BRIEF CONTEST SCHEDULE

June 14, 2019: Arrival and Registration (Transportation will be arranged from New Delhi and Hazrat Nizamuddin stations only)
June 15, 2019: Practice (About 10 mins in each color field. Exact schedule will be available upon arrival)
June 16, 2019: Preliminaries, QFs, SFs, and Final

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Technical Report required in Stage 2?
No Technical Report is required in Stage 2 but read "Stage 2 Details" carefully.
What if there are some changes in the number of team members after registering?
You cannot add any new member after you registered and paid by the last date. In case, somebody cannot make to DD-Robocon 2019 we are really sorry.
In a scenario like a team member is not opting for accommodation will he/she gets a certificate?
Those students whose names were provided by the institute/college authority and uploaded are eligible for the certificate provided they participate during the competition days and show their college I-card. Every participant MUST carry their college I-card along with the registration badge which will be checked during registration on June 14, 2019.
When the practice field will be available?
See Brief Contest Schedule above
Is there faculty accommodation along with the team members?
Yes, it was arranged in the hostels provided they registered along with the team members and paid. Sorry, no separate accommodation is available for the faculty members. If they have not already registered with their team members and paid we will not be able to provide any accommodation.
Will other students be allowed to watch DD-Robocon 2019 from participating universities?
Not in the contest arena. It was mainly due to large registrations by the teams. Sorry for the strictness. However, we are trying to work out video feed of the games in a separate lecture hall or web. If it materializes then one can enjoy the games without being in the game arena physically.
When the registration will be done at I.I.T Delhi?
Upon arrival on June 14, 2019. The exact venue will be informed soon.
Transportation facilities to teams not coming to 2 stations we have mentioned on websites. Some could not find train tickets. So they are planning to come by bus.
Sorry, you have to arrange to come directly to the Registration venue of IIT Campus on June 14, 2019. Exact details will be informed later.
